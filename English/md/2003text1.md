## 一、文章中英文主旨
### 中文主旨
本文以二战传奇美国间谍大师比尔·多诺万对情报工具的开放包容态度作为引入，阐述互联网推动传统间谍行业变革、催生开源情报产业的发展现状；以行业头部私营情报企业Straitford为典型案例，介绍其盈利模式、线上运营方式、团队配置特点，点明开源情报存在信息真伪难辨的行业痛点，最终阐明Straitford依靠体制外独立立场打造核心竞争力、实现行业成功的原因。

### 英文主旨
Taking Wild Bill Donovan, a legendary American spymaster who embraced all accessible espionage tools in his era, as the lead-in, the article illustrates how the Internet transforms traditional espionage and gives birth to the open-source intelligence industry. It takes the leading private intelligence firm Straitford as a typical case to introduce its profit model, online operation and team features, points out the pain point of distinguishing reliable information from false content in open-source spying, and finally explains how Straitford gains long-term success with its out-of-establishment independent stance.

**长难句结构分析**：
主句主干为`The American spymaster was fascinated with information`；

**长难句说明**：动名词`using...`作believe in的宾语，宾语中嵌套省略that的定语从句`(that) came to hand`修饰tools；破折号后内容是对前文间谍博弈的补充解释。

**常考单词&短语**：
- remake（re-made）：v. 重塑、改造
- pastime：n. 休闲消遣活动

## 三、完整原文复刻（与原图分段格式完全一致）
Wild Bill Donovan would have loved the Internet. The American spymaster who built the Office of Strategic Services in the World War II and later laid the roots for the CIA was fascinated with information. Donovan believed in using whatever tools came to hand in the "great game" of espionage — spying as a "profession." These days the Net, which has already re-made such everyday pastimes as buying books and sending mail, is reshaping Donovan's vocation as well.

The latest revolution isn't simply a matter of gentlemen reading other gentlemen's e-mail. That kind of electronic spying has been going on for decades. In the past three or four years, the World Wide Web has given birth to a whole industry of point-and-click spying. The spooks call it "open source intelligence," and as the Net grows, it is becoming increasingly influential. In 1995 the CIA held a contest to see who could compile the most data about Burundi. The winner, by a large margin, was a tiny Virginia company called Open-Source Solutions, whose clear advantage was its mastery of the electronic world.

Among the firms making the biggest splash in the new world is Straitford, Inc., a private intelligence-analysis firm based in Austin, Texas. Straitford makes money by selling the results of spying (covering nations from Chile to Russia) to corporations like energy-services firm McDermott International. Many of its predictions are available online at www.Straitford.com.

Straitford president George Friedman says he sees the online world as a kind of mutually reinforcing tool for both information collection and distribution, a spymaster's dream. Last week his firm was busy vacuuming up data bits from the far corners of the world and predicting a crisis in Ukraine. "As soon as that report runs, we'll suddenly get 500 new internet sign-ups from Ukraine," says Friedman, a former political science professor. "And we'll hear back from some of them." Open-source spying does have its risks, of course, since it can be difficult to tell good information from bad. That's where Straitford earns its keep.

Friedman relies on a lean staff of 20 in Austin. Several of his staff members have military-intelligence backgrounds. He sees the firm's outsider status as the key to its success. Straitford's briefs don't sound like the usual Washington back-and-forthing, whereby agencies avoid dramatic declarations on the chance they might be wrong. Straitford, says Friedman, takes pride in its independent voice.

## 四、全文完整通顺翻译
传奇间谍比尔·多诺万如果身处当下，一定会深深喜爱互联网。这位美国情报大师在二战期间创立了战略情报局，后续又为美国中央情报局（CIA）筑牢了发展根基，他一生都对情报工作充满热忱。多诺万秉持一个理念：在间谍这场“宏大博弈”中，所有能利用的工具都要尽数用上，他将间谍活动视作一门正经职业。如今互联网早已改变了网购书籍、线上寄信这类日常休闲活动，眼下它也正在重塑多诺万当年深耕的情报行业。

这场最新的行业变革，绝不只是体面人偷看他人邮件这么简单。这类电子监控类间谍活动已经开展了数十年，但在近三四年里，万维网催生出了一整套依托鼠标点击操作的全新间谍产业。情报从业者将其命名为“开源情报”，随着互联网规模不断扩大，这类情报模式的影响力也持续提升。1995年，美国中央情报局举办了一场竞赛，比拼谁能整理出最详尽的布隆迪相关资料。最终一家名叫“开源解决方案”的弗吉尼亚小型企业以巨大优势夺冠，它最突出的竞争力，就是对电子网络领域的纯熟把控。

在这个新赛道里风头正盛的企业中，斯特拉特福德公司格外亮眼，这是一家总部设在得克萨斯州奥斯汀市的私营情报分析机构。斯特拉特福德的盈利模式是把覆盖从智利到俄罗斯多国的情报调研成果，卖给麦克德莫特国际能源服务公司这类企业客户，它发布的多数预测分析内容，都能在官网www.Straitford.com在线查看。

斯特拉特福德的总裁乔治·弗里德曼表示，在他看来，网络世界是能让信息收集和分发相辅相成的工具，这正是情报从业者梦寐以求的东西。上周，他的公司正从全球各地搜集零散数据，预判乌克兰即将爆发危机。曾担任政治学教授的弗里德曼说：“这份报告一经发布，我们马上就会收获500个来自乌克兰的网站新注册用户，其中一部分人还会向我们反馈信息。”当然，开源情报模式本身存在风险——人们很难分辨信息的真伪优劣，而这恰恰是斯特拉特福德能立足行业、站稳脚跟的核心优势。

弗里德曼在奥斯汀只维持着一支20人的精简团队，团队里多名成员都有军事情报从业经历。他认为企业身处体制外的独立定位，是成功的关键。斯特拉特福德的情报简报文风，和华盛顿官方机构惯有的来回推诿风格完全不同：官方机构生怕判断失误，会刻意避免发布激进的公开言论；而弗里德曼称，斯特拉特福德最引以为傲的，就是自己始终保持独立中立的发声立场。
